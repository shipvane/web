The difference

Blind AI codegen ships broken code.

Most AI coding tools generate code they can never run. Shipvane works in a real checkout and proves its work against your actual build.

Without Shipvane

Generated, not verified

The model writes files it can't execute, guesses at your conventions, and hands you a PR that may not even compile. You become the build server.

With Shipvane

Implemented, then proven

Shipvane edits a real branch, runs your real build & tests, reads the actual errors, and iterates until the gate is green. It isn't done until CI passes.
